About Kaotheem

Specialized Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) service company committed to ensuring the safety, reliability, and integrity of industrial assets across Nigeria.

Our Story

Over 9 Years of Excellence in NDT Services

Kaotheem Energy Services Ltd was founded with a clear vision: to provide exceptional Non-Destructive Testing services that ensure the safety and reliability of critical industrial assets. Since our establishment, we have grown into a trusted partner for major players in the oil & gas, construction, manufacturing, power, and transportation sectors across Nigeria.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to precision, safety, and quality. We understand that every component matters, and our certified NDT professionals utilize state-of-the-art technology to deliver accurate, efficient, and standards-compliant testing solutions.

Our reputation has been built on delivering outstanding results with integrity and expertise. We take pride in helping our clients minimize risk, reduce downtime, and extend the life of their valuable assets.
