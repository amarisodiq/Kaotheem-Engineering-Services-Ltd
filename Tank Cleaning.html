Tank Cleaning & Desludging Services

Restoring tank capacity and operational efficiency

Kaotheem Energy Services provides professional tank cleaning and desludging services to maintain operational efficiency and extend asset life. Our experienced team uses advanced cleaning techniques and equipment to safely remove sludge, sediment, and hydrocarbon deposits from storage tanks.

We specialize in crude oil tank cleaning, storage tank desludging, and industrial container cleaning. Our API 653 compliant procedures ensure safe and effective cleaning operations with minimal downtime.
