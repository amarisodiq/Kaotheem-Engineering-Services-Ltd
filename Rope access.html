Rope Access

At Kaotheem Engineering Services, we offer safe, efficient, and cost-effective Rope Access solutions for high-level and difficult-to-reach locations. Our rope access technicians are IRATA-certified and trained to perform inspections, maintenance, repairs, and installations at height or in confined spacesâ€”without the need for expensive scaffolding or heavy equipment.

Our Rope Access services include:
  • Structural inspections and surveys
  • NDT and visual testing at height
  • Welding and mechanical repairs
  • Painting and coating applications
  • Bolt torquing and flange management
  • Cleaning, sealing, and maintenance of tall structures
Rope Access is ideal for:
  • Offshore platforms and rigs
  • Wind turbines and telecommunication towers
  • Bridges, silos, and chimneys
  • High-rise buildings and industrial facilities
  • Ship hulls and marine structures
