Hull & Structural Inspection Services

Ensuring safety and reliability of marine and structural assets

Kaotheem delivers precise hull and structural inspection services to assess the condition of vessels, offshore platforms, bridges, and critical infrastructure. Our certified inspectors use both visual and advanced NDT techniques to detect damage, corrosion, and fatigue — ensuring safety, compliance, and extended service life.

We specialize in marine vessel surveys, offshore platform assessments, and industrial structural inspections. Our comprehensive approach helps clients identify potential failures before they occur, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

ABS Certified DNV Standards API RP 2A
