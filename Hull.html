Hull Cleaning

At Kaotheem Engineering Services, we offer efficient and environmentally responsible Hull Cleaning services to improve vessel performance, reduce fuel consumption, and extend the life of your marine assets. Over time, hulls accumulate marine growth such as algae, barnacles, and other biofouling, which can increase drag and negatively impact operational efficiency.

How We Work
Our skilled team uses advanced tools and diver-operated or ROV (remotely operated vehicle) cleaning systems to safely remove buildup from vessel hullsâ€”without damaging protective coatings. All cleaning is done following international environmental and safety guidelines.

Our Hull Cleaning Services Include:
  • Underwater and dry-dock hull cleaning
  • Removal of marine growth and scaling
  • Propeller and rudder cleaning
  • In-water video inspections before and after cleaning
  • Biofouling assessment and documentation
