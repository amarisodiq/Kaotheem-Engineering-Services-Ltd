Hull Cleaning Services

Restoring vessel performance and extending operational life

Kaotheem offers professional hull cleaning services to improve vessel performance, fuel efficiency, and lifespan. Our experienced team safely removes marine growth and fouling using eco-friendly methods, helping reduce drag, prevent corrosion, and maintain operational standards.

Regular hull cleaning is essential for maintaining vessel efficiency and reducing fuel consumption. Marine growth such as barnacles, algae, and other organisms can significantly increase drag, leading to higher fuel costs and reduced speed. Our specialized cleaning techniques ensure optimal hull condition with minimal environmental impact.
