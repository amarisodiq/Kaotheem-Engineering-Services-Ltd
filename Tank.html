Tank Inspection Services

Ensuring the safety and reliability of your storage tanks

Kaotheem provides comprehensive tank inspection services to ensure the structural integrity, safety, and compliance of storage tanks. Using both conventional and advanced NDT methods, we detect corrosion, cracks, and other defects early — helping you avoid costly failures and maintain operational efficiency.

Our certified inspectors follow API 653, API 650, and API 510 standards to deliver accurate assessments and actionable recommendations. We serve the oil & gas, petrochemical, chemical, and power generation industries across Nigeria.

Inspection Methods

Advanced NDT techniques for thorough tank assessment

Ultrasonic Thickness Measurement (UTM)

Accurate wall thickness measurements to identify corrosion and erosion

Corrosion Mapping

Detailed mapping of corrosion patterns and remaining wall thickness

Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL)

Rapid scanning of tank floors for corrosion and pitting

Visual Inspection

Internal and external visual assessment with CCTV and drone technology

Vacuum Box Testing

Leak detection for tank bottoms and shell welds

Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT)

Advanced weld inspection and corrosion assessment
