NDT Consumables Supplier in Nigeria

Your Trusted Source for NDT Consumables

Kaotheem Energy Services Ltd is a leading NDT consumables supplier in Nigeria, providing high-performance materials, chemicals, and accessories used in Penetrant Testing (PT) and Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI). Our solutions meet international standards and support reliable inspection across diverse industries including oil & gas, fabrication, power, and manufacturing.

Authorized Distributor for SREM Technologies

As an authorized distributor in Nigeria, we supply PT & MPI consumables developed by SREM TECHNOLOGIES, manufactured under strict quality controls and certified to ISO 9001:2015 for quality management.
