Asset Integrity Management

Protecting your critical assets from failure

Kaotheem provides end-to-end Asset Integrity Management services to help clients maintain the safety, reliability, and performance of critical assets throughout their lifecycle. We combine inspection, monitoring, and risk assessment to minimize downtime, extend asset life, and ensure full regulatory compliance.

Our integrated approach combines advanced inspection technologies, engineering analysis, and data management to provide a complete picture of asset condition. We help clients optimize maintenance strategies, reduce operational risks, and make informed decisions about asset investments.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive integrity management solutions

Risk-Based Inspection (RBI)

Optimized inspection planning based on risk assessment:

  • API 580/581 compliant RBI methodology
  • Probability of failure analysis
  • Consequence of failure assessment
  • Optimized inspection intervals
  • Risk mitigation strategies

Fitness for Service (FFS)

Engineering assessment of damaged equipment:

  • ASME FFS-1 / API 579 compliant
  • Corrosion and damage assessment
  • Remaining strength evaluation
  • Crack and flaw assessment
  • Run-repair-replace decisions

Corrosion Monitoring

Continuous tracking of corrosion activity:

  • Electrical resistance (ER) probes
  • Linear polarization resistance (LPR)
  • Ultrasonic thickness monitoring
  • Corrosion coupon analysis
  • Real-time data acquisition
