Helideck Inspection Services

Ensuring safe helicopter operations on FPSOs, platforms, and vessels

At Kaotheem Energy Services Limited, we deliver comprehensive helideck inspection services in line with international aviation safety standards, ensuring safe helicopter operations on offshore and onshore facilities such as FPSOs, platforms, and vessels.

We combine industry expertise, advanced inspection techniques, and strict adherence to international standards (such as ICAO and CAP 437 guidelines) to deliver reliable and accurate helideck inspection services. Our goal is simple: to ensure every helideck we inspect is safe, compliant, and ready for operation.

Project Highlight

Helideck Inspection – Usan FPSO

Pull Test

Tie-Down Point Strength Test

We carried out pull tests on helideck tie-down points to determine their load-bearing capacity and structural integrity. This test verifies that each tie-down point can safely secure helicopters during parking and under extreme environmental conditions, including high wind loads and operational stresses.

Friction Test

Slip Resistance Test

To guarantee safe landing and movement of helicopters, we measured the surface friction of the helideck. This ensures compliance with required skid resistance levels, especially in wet or contaminated conditions.

Drop Test

Impact Resistance Assessment

We conducted controlled drop tests to assess the impact resistance and structural response of the helideck surface. This verifies the deck's ability to withstand dynamic loading during helicopter landing operations.

Additional Helideck Inspection Services

Full suite of helideck inspection and certification services

Visual Inspection

Detailed examination of helideck surfaces, markings, lighting systems, and overall structural condition

Helideck Net Inspection

Assessment of safety nets for integrity, proper installation, and compliance with aviation standards

Lighting System Inspection

Verification of helideck lighting, including perimeter lights and approach lighting, to ensure visibility and compliance with aviation authority requirements

Markings and Paint Compliance Check

Inspection of helideck markings (e.g., "H" marking, perimeter lines) for accuracy, visibility, and adherence to regulatory standards

Drainage Assessment

Evaluation of helideck drainage systems to prevent water accumulation, which could compromise safety

Structural Integrity Assessment

Inspection of the helideck structure for corrosion, deformation, or fatigue using appropriate NDT techniques where necessary

Obstacle Limitation Survey (OLS)

Identification of potential obstructions within the helicopter approach and take-off path to ensure safe operations
